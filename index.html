About Me

I'm a learning User Experience Designer who loves building web and mobile applications. I have got a year of experience working with two products of Paragon Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd. i.e Digivalet, and CasaDigi. Over my year, I have created interactive features for the web, iPad and iPhone applications from building wireframing to prototyping of end screens. During my internship, I had experience creating various advertising elements like logos, posters, banners, gifs, email templates, social media promotion posts and other mock-ups for t-shirts, cups, stickers.

I am a tech-enthusiast who never gave up learning upon technologies. My passion for planning and designing products arose more after visiting the BITs Pilani summit, Rajasthan Hackathon 4.0 and joining different communities like Google Developers Group, Facebook Developers Circle, GirlScript and Women Techmakers, Indore.

See My Resume !

For the past 6 months, I've been teaching myself about UX interviews and usability testing. I believe in project-based learning and spend time making stuff.
